About Us

About the Company

We are Candelaria, a leading manufacturer and marketer of aromatic candles established in 2015.


Our Story

We opened our first factory in 2015 and started producing 375 of candle varieties. Over time we expanded our footprint to cover local and international markets.


Our Vision

To delight customers by providing products of exceptional quality and pleasant fragrances that promote relaxation and ambiance.


Our Process

We use only the finest raw materials like natural fragrance oils and virgin wax to ensure superior quality. We also focus on innovative designs, shapes and colors to suit diverse tastes.
